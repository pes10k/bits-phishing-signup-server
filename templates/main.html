Thank You For Your Involvement!

Thank you very much for agreeing to be a part of this security. The next step of the process is to select a location and date that you are able to meet to formally enroll in the study.

During this meeting we will go over the purpose of the study and review the necessary qualifications each participant meet to participate. You will also be asked to sign piece of paper, stating that you agree to participate in the study.

You will then be guided through installing a browser extension in your browser, which you will need to allow run for the next two months. You must bring the laptop you use as your primary web-browsing device to this meeting. Failure to do so will make you ineligible to participate in the study.

You will be able to ask questions at any time during this meeting. The meeting will last approximately 30 minutes. Once the meeting is over you will receive your initial $15 Amazon Gift Card. You will receive the second $15 gift card on your successful completion of the study.
